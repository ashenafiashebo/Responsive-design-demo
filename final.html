Sheikh Hussein Jibril prophecy

May 8, 2011 in Category

Most of his poems were predictions about the upcoming rulers of Ethiopia and sociopolitical situations of the country starting from the times of Zemene Mesfaent (Era of the Princes, of1769) to the present regime. For one who knows Ethiopian history well, the prophetic poems hit a 100% right. The poems are colloquial in writing and double-edged in meaning so they need to be read between the lines and with a deep knowledge of the Amharic language,Ethiopian history and religions. For eg, he terms Christianity as Amara instead of calling it Christianity and similar kinds of usages..
